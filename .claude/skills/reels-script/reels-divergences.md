# Reels — Divergences from TikTok

Disclosed reference for `reels-script`. Only what differs from the TikTok script conventions; the shared short-form mechanics (hook-first, beat structure, muted-viewing on-screen text) carry over unchanged.

## Aesthetic

- Reels skews slightly more polished/aesthetic than TikTok's raw register — cleaner framing and color are rewarded more, though authenticity still beats over-production.
- Instagram's audience overlaps but trends marginally older and more lifestyle/aspirational; pitch the register accordingly.

## Audio sourcing

- Reels uses Instagram's own audio library, not TikTok's. A sound trending on TikTok may not exist or trend on Reels — source trending audio from Reels specifically (via the `trend-research` signal for Instagram, not TikTok data).
- Original audio and voiceover carry well on Reels.

## Distribution

- Reels leans on Explore and hashtag/keyword discovery more than TikTok's pure FYP model — the first frame and cover matter for the grid.
- Length tolerance is similar (~15–60s); the same one-point compression applies.

## What does NOT change

- Hook-first restructuring, the beat spine, one idea per beat, muted-viewing on-screen text, native register over polish. Do not re-derive these — they come from the shared short-form method.
