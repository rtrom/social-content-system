# Threads — Divergences from X

Disclosed reference for `threads-copy`. Why a Threads post must not mirror the X post.

## Length and format

- Threads posts run to 500 characters — nearly double X's default. Use the room; a post that breathes fits Threads better than a compressed one-liner.
- Threads tolerates a multi-sentence post that unfolds an idea, where X rewards the single sharp line.

## Register

- More conversational, personal, community-facing. Threads skews toward discussion and reply, less toward the declarative hot take X rewards.
- A post that invites a response (genuinely, not engagement-bait) fits the platform's reply-heavy culture.

## Algorithm / distribution

- Threads surfaces more on interest and follow graphs and rewards replies and ongoing conversation; a post that starts a thread of genuine replies travels.
- Less real-time/news-cycle-driven than X — evergreen framing works.

## What to carry, what to drop

- **Carry:** the pillar's core message and the brief's CTA.
- **Drop:** X's compression, its declarative punch, and above all the literal X wording. Derive from the pillar afresh.
